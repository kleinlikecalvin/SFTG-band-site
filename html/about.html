A Gruesome History

In the beginning of time, literally (this band is old as shit), a man who is upright in more ways than just bass named Fabian Grave and previous/forever honorary member/BFFL named Demonted Tony were drawn towards the sweet and sensual music of subcultures. They joined forces with other friends to create the band of their nightmares, Straight From The Grave.

Around their original stomping grounds of Anaheim, they played many a show and ran through many a member. BUT! They never let the inconsistencies of the band stand in the way of their passion for playing sick (and I mean sick) jams for any ears willing to listen.

Flash forward past some haituses and more unforseen circumstances... Straight From The Grave finally settled into a solid line-up which included guitar maestro Alex Sin and drum god Nikki Dixxx. With such a face melting combination of musicians SFTG was finally ready to get super serious. They almost immediately went to work writing a full length album and revamping old favorites from SFTG's lengthy history. Once the jams had been perfected and tested on the mindless zombies they enslaved with their charms, they put out a full length album (which will be available in the online store soon).

With a new album as fiery as the depths of hell, Straight From The Grave played a gajillion new sick fests and shows with local groups like Dante and The Hellhounds, Mask, and Catacomb Rockers! Eventually Demonted Tony returned to hell to continue serving Satan for the rest of his days along with his five gremlins. Following his departure, Nikki Dixxx was possessed by the seemingly indestructable spirit of Nosos itself. While it was unfortunate to see her fade into sick life, THE SHOW MUST GO ON! So Straight From The Grave brought in Andy Suicide and the show did in fact go on. Unfortunately, for more reasons than there is space to address, COVID-19 cut their winning streak abruptly short. But don't fret! They still have many plans for the future and are VERY open to accepting new and creative opportunities to persist during the foreseeable future!
